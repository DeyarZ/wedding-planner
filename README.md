# Wedding Planner App - Revolutionary UI

A wedding planning app with unprecedented UI/UX that redefines mobile app interaction through emotional intelligence and creative visualization.

## Revolutionary UI Concepts

### 🌀 Ring Navigation
- **Circular wedding ring interface** - Rotate to navigate between sections
- **Gesture-driven** - Natural spinning motion to switch contexts
- **Dynamic center void** - Always shows countdown in the middle
- **Magnetic snapping** - Sections lock into place smoothly

### 🎭 Emotion-Responsive Design
- **Breathing background gradients** - Shift colors based on stress levels
- **Floating emotion orb** - Pulses to indicate current state
- **Adaptive UI morphing** - Interface complexity adapts to user stress
- **Stress-based navigation** - Most urgent items surface automatically

### 📖 Timeline as Physical Pages
- **Page-flip metaphor** - Swipe through timeline like a wedding album
- **3D rotation effects** - Pages rotate in space as you browse
- **Progressive disclosure** - Tasks animate in as you land on each page
- **Visual progress** - Each page shows completion as a growing bar

### 🃏 Vendor Deck Interface
- **Tinder-style swiping** - Swipe right to like, left to pass, up to super-like
- **Stacked card visualization** - See upcoming vendors behind current
- **Instant actions** - Portfolio, message, and book without leaving the card
- **Category color coding** - Each vendor type has its own gradient

### 🌌 Guest Constellation
- **Relationship mapping** - Guests positioned based on connections
- **Interactive nodes** - Size indicates party size, glow shows importance
- **Constellation lines** - Visual connections between related guests
- **Starfield background** - Creates dreamy, cosmic atmosphere
- **Drag to reorganize** - Physically move guests to different groups

### 🌻 Budget Garden
- **Flowers as categories** - Each budget category grows as a unique flower
- **Health visualization** - Flowers wilt when overspending, bloom when healthy
- **Day/night cycle** - Sun/moon position reflects overall budget health
- **Interactive watering** - "Water" categories to allocate more funds
- **Organic growth** - Flowers sway and grow based on spending patterns

## Key Innovations

1. **No traditional navigation bar** - Ring replaces tabs completely
2. **Emotion-first design** - UI responds to wedding planning stress
3. **Physical metaphors** - Pages flip, cards shuffle, flowers grow
4. **Spatial organization** - Guests in space, timeline as journey
5. **Living interface** - Everything breathes, pulses, and responds

## Running the Project

1. Open `WeddingPlanner.xcodeproj` in Xcode
2. Select iPhone simulator (designed for iPhone 14 Pro and above)
3. Press Cmd+R to build and run
4. Experience the revolutionary interface

## Gesture Guide

- **Ring Navigation**: Drag in circle to rotate between sections
- **Timeline**: Swipe left/right to flip pages
- **Vendors**: Swipe cards left (reject), right (like), up (super like)
- **Guests**: Pinch to zoom constellation, drag to pan
- **Budget**: Tap flowers to see details, tap water button to allocate funds

## Design Philosophy

This isn't just an app—it's an emotional companion that understands the stress of wedding planning. Every interaction is designed to feel natural, reduce anxiety, and make planning feel like magic rather than work.
